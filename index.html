♠ HOW TO PLAY BLACKJACK

Objective

Blackjack is a card game where the goal is to beat the dealer by having a hand value closer to 21 without exceeding it. Players compete against the dealer, not each other. A hand of an Ace and a 10, Jack, Queen, or King is a Blackjack.

Card Values
2 – 10Face value
J, Q, K10 points each
Ace1 or 11 points
Gameplay

Placing Bets: Place bets before cards are dealt (minimum 🪙 25).

Dealing: You receive two cards face-up. Dealer gets one face-up, one face-down.

Player Actions
  • Hit — Take another card. Keep hitting below 21. Exceed 21 and you bust.
  • Stand — Keep your hand and end your turn.
  • Double Down — Double your bet, receive exactly one more card.
  • Split — Split a pair into two hands, each with an equal additional bet.
  • Insurance — Side bet if dealer shows Ace. Pays 2:1 if dealer has blackjack.
  • Surrender — Forfeit and recover half your bet (available after initial deal).
Dealer Rules
  • Dealer must hit until reaching 17 or higher.
  • If the dealer busts, all remaining players win.
  • If dealer is closer to 21, dealer wins. Equal scores = Push.
